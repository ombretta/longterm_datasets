The results of this study will be used for research purposes and for the creation of research publications. All responses are treated as confidential. Your participation in this research is voluntary and we do not collect any personal information from the participants, therefore your contribution cannot be withdrawn after the completion of this study.
If you understand the statements above and freely consent to participate in the task, please answer the following questions and press the 'Submit' button. If you do not wish to participate, then please do not click on the 'Submit' button.

In this task we ask you to watch one video clip and express what is the relationship between the characters in the video clip, in your opinion. There are 3 possible relationships:

  1. Friends
  2. Husband-Wife
  3. Boyfriend-Girlfriend

To select a relationship, click on the corresponding button. When you are in doubt about which relationship to select, choose the button I am not sure.
It is important that you watch the video clip from the beginning to the end. You will be able to click on the options only after you finished watching the full video clip.
We use a mild attention check to accept or reject your work.
Your input will be used to understand how to improve automatic video understanding.
